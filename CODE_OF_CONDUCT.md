# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering an open, welcoming, inclusive, and respectful community where everyone can participate constructively.

We pledge to make participation in our projects and community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Demonstrating empathy and respect.
- Being constructive during discussions and code reviews.
- Accepting feedback professionally.
- Focusing on collaboration and shared learning.
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language.
- Personal attacks or insulting comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or projects.
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional environment.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, or other contributions that violate this policy.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all KryoNex public repositories, discussions, issues, pull requests, and other official community spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact:

support@kryonex.live

All reports will be reviewed appropriately and handled with discretion.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take appropriate action in response to violations, including warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent removal from participation when necessary.
